Steganography, what is it?

Steganography is the art of hiding secret data in plain sight. It sounds kind of counter-intuitive, but you’d be surprised how effective it is. Hiding things such as source code, passwords, IP addresses, and other confidential information in pictures, music, or other random files tends to be the last place anyone would think of finding them. You should note that steganography and cryptography are not mutually exclusive from each other. One may contain elements of the other or both.
